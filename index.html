Skopos Theory

According to Hans Vermeer, translation is mainly an intention-based work. In memes, "fidelity to the original" is secondary in relation to "fidelity to the purpose."

▼ Read more

Priority of pragmatics. If the goal of a meme is to make a local community laugh, complete deconstruction of the source text is permitted.

Flexibility of rules. It's okay to redo the tone, swap characters or cultural realities.

Situation analysis. Determine whether the meme is informative or purely attractive.

📊 78% find memified format of academic instructions motivating.
🎭

Multimodality

The translator works not only with text, but with all channels of meaning transmission. A meme is a single semiotic object.

▼ Read more

Third meaning. Text and images create a "third meaning" together.

Visual Anchoring. Text must explain the character's expression.

Spatial limitation. Translation should not clutter the frame.
